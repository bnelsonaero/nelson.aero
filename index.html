Flight Instruction Services

Private & Commercial Pilot Training

Flight Reviews

Go beyond the minimums. A comprehensive review of your skills and knowledge to ensure you remain a safe and proficient pilot.

Expert guidance when transitioning to a new make or model. I provide checkout flights for Cessna, Piper, Cirrus, and other general aviation aircraft, helping you master the systems, handling characteristics, and avionics.

Endorsements

Expand your privileges with High Performance and Complex endorsements. Gain the skills to operate faster, more capable aircraft safely.

Checkride Prep

Ground Instruction

About Bryce Nelson

I am a native of New Orleans with 17 years of experience in the aviation software & data industry and as a pilot. As a Certificated Flight Instructor (CFI), my passion is sharing the freedom and discipline of flight.

I believe in a personalized approach to training, ensuring each student not only meets the requirements but develops the judgement and skills of a safe, competent pilot. I provide flight instruction throughout Southeast Louisiana and the Gulf South, including New Orleans, Hammond, Slidell, Covington, and Baton Rouge. I am available at airports including KNEW, KHDC, KAPS, KBTR, KASD, and surrounding fields.

Whether you are pursuing a career in aviation or fulfilling a lifelong dream to fly, I am dedicated to guiding you through every takeoff and landing on your path to success.
